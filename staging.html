For everyone

We define a community as someplace where everyone is invited, everyone is included, and everyone is listened to. We love the music but our passion doesn’t stop there, our community is about connecting people from all over the world, regardless of who you are and what you like.

At your service

Our dedicated team of staff, including our very own community helper, “Bot”, are at your service. We’re ready to answer any questions you might have, or just do what we do best, keep the community alive with passion and entertainment for you to enjoy.

Friendly community

We’re one of the oldest communities on plug.dj and we wouldn’t have gotten this far if it wasn’t for the friendly atmosphere our community never fails to deliver. We are always welcoming of new guests and encourage them to engage in the community and meet some new friends.
